About Us

At Seed Cabs & Tours, we take pride in being the top choice for travelers and locals seeking reliable, affordable, and comfortable transportation. Located in matara, Sri Lanka our mission is to provide a seamless car rental experience that ensures your journey is as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

Our Services

  • Normal Rent

    Perfect for daily transportation needs, whether it’s commuting, running errands, or exploring the city. Offers a wide range of vehicles, from compact cars to SUVs, ensuring comfort and convenience for both short-term and long-term rentals.

  • Wedding Rent

    Elevate your special day with luxurious and stylish wedding car rentals. Choose from classic, vintage, or modern luxury vehicles, ensuring a grand entrance and unforgettable memories. These cars add elegance and sophistication to your celebration.

  • Airport Rent

    Designed for seamless airport transfers, this service offers quick and easy access to a variety of vehicles right at the terminal. airport car rentals provide a comfortable and stress-free way to reach your next destination, with flexible pick-up and drop-off options.

Terms & Condition

  • Vehicle Rental Liability

    "The renter assumes full responsibility for any illegal use of the rented vehicle and agrees to pay fees if the vehicle is seized."

  • Driver Liability and Indemnity

    "The renter agrees to personally cover any damages caused by allowing another person to drive the rented vehicle."

  • Vehicle Maintenance

    "The renter agrees to properly maintain the vehicle and ensure all necessary documents, such as the revenue license and insurance, are kept up to date."

  • Damage and Loss Liability

    "The renter agrees to cover any loss or damage to the vehicle during their possession, either through personal funds or insurance, and will bear the cost of any damage not covered by the insurance."

  • Rental Payment and Extended Tax

    "The renter agrees to pay the full daily tax for the first 14 days and half the daily tax for each additional day until the completion of the new Twadiya.

  • Accident and Breakdown Cost Liability

    "The renter agrees to cover all costs incurred by the company in the event of an accident, towing, or loading due to a vehicle breakdown."

  • Deposit Withholding and Loss Assessment

    "The renter acknowledges that the deposit will not be refunded until the institution assesses and deducts any charges for damages or losses resulting from an accident."
